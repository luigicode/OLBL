The Hardworking People of OLBL office

I went to church and visited the parish office of OLBL.
There I met 2 friendly secretaries, Ate Ronaly and Kuya Michael
(Ate Neda wasn't there).
Although they were very busy, they were willing to assist me.
They even told me about what they do in the office
(their roles and responsibilities).


Their duties in the office vary with small things, handling of files relating to the OLBL church or its members, managing the bulletin board,
to bigger events like fundraising or children's mass.


They also help people with personal things such as marriage documents, baptismal appointments, funeral services,
or simple things like the schedule of mass or directions.


The parish office plays an important role in the church.
Without them, we probably wouldn't know where the restroom is.
I do suggest you visit them when you have the time.
They will greet you with a friendly face and help you in any way they can.

Luigi Isberto

YLSE 24
